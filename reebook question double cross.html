Product Details


The Reebok Question gets a new look in 2019 with the upcoming ‘Double Cross’ release. One of my favorite basketball shoes of all-time is re-released in a way that actually disappointed me. Leather isn’t as nice as it usually is while vinyl makes an appearance in areas that its never been before. Reebok did add a bit more Hexalite cushioning in the heel, but the classic shock pad PU insole has been replaced with a cheap thin insole. One of the areas I feel was updated in a good way is the toe box. The shape is much sleeker and looks great. This change hasn’t altered the fit at all which is a huge plus.
