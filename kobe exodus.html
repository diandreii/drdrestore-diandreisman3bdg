Product Details


Reviewers tend to like the latest release of Kobes AD signature model, but they don't love it. The traction of the Kobe AD Exodus works very well on clean courts but attracts a lot of dust and isn't made for outdoor use. The huge 14mm Nike Zoom Air cushioning in the heel is very comfortable and the Phylon in the forefoot is low-to-the-ground and responsive. Support and lockdown are solid and the materials may look and feel cheap in-hand, but they get the job done! Overall, the Kobe AD Exodus is a very solid performance shoe that is especially recommended for quicker guards.
